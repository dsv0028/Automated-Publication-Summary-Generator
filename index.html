How it works

Our solution helps simplify the process of summarizing faculty research. By uploading a file with author names, the tool automatically retrieves publication data from multiple sources and generates key metrics.

1. Upload Your File

Submit the Author Names in Excel File or enter a single Author name.

2. Summarizes The Publications

Refine the AI-generated summary with additional prompts for more focused or detailed results.

3. Download and Share

Download the summary as a PDF or share it online with a unique URL.

Why choose My Publication Summarizer?

Upload Data Files

Interactive Graphs
